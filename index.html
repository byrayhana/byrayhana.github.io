# About

I'm an applied AI research engineer at NewMind AI (Istanbul), working across the full LLM lifecycle: pretraining & fine-tuning, high-performance multi-node inference, retrieval / RAG, evaluation, and agentic systems — specialized in Turkish and legal-domain NLP.

I've pretrained Turkish language models on the BSC MareNostrum 5 supercomputer, stood up and benchmarked around twenty frontier model families across quantization formats, and co-authored research on Turkish information retrieval and multi-agent systems. My work is measurement-driven — I care about calibration, fair benchmarking protocols, and honest negative results. I'm comfortable from CUDA/driver-level serving all the way up to shipping applications.

# Selected Work

Turkish Diffusion Language Models

Pretrained 1.7B-parameter Turkish diffusion language models at multiple context lengths on the BSC MareNostrum 5 supercomputer, and built an evaluation harness measuring diffusion-ELBO perplexity, generation quality, and context-scaling degradation.

PyTorchMegatron / MegaDLMsHPCTokenizer design

Frontier-Model Inference & Serving

Stood up and benchmarked ~20 frontier model families (Qwen3 / 3.5, Kimi K2, DeepSeek-V4, MiniMax, NVIDIA Nemotron, StepFun, Gemma-4, GLM…) across vLLM & SGLang and FP8 / NVFP4 / GGUF quantization, including multi-node MoE serving and a CUDA forward-compatibility fix.

vLLMSGLangQuantizationMulti-node GPU

TurkColBERT — Late-Interaction Retrieval

Co-authored the first benchmark of dense vs. late-interaction retrievers for Turkish information retrieval. A 1.0M-parameter late-interaction model — ~600× smaller than a 600M dense encoder — retained >71% of its mAP. Models released open-source.

ColBERT / PyLateInformation RetrievalACLing 2025

Multi-Agent RAG & Agentology

Co-authored research on ontology-driven multi-agent systems (Panoptic Knowledge Precomputation) that replaces query-time retrieval with a single precompute pass, and built hierarchical multi-agent RAG pipelines for document analysis.

Multi-agentMCPRAGOntology

LLM Evaluation Methodology

Designed benchmark and evaluation harnesses using asymmetry-of-verification task design, adversarial difficulty filtering, calibration (Brier / ECE), and LLM-as-judge grading — used to qualify models before production.

BenchmarkingCalibrationLLM-as-judge

Retrieval & Semantic Caching

Built a multi-layer semantic cache for RAG pipelines, and reimplemented & benchmarked from scratch the LEANN low-storage vector index against FAISS Flat and IVF-PQ, quantifying the storage / latency trade-off.
