About

I'm an AI Research Engineer at NewMind AI, where my work spans research and production engineering for large language models. I have pretrained Turkish language models on the Barcelona Supercomputing Center's MareNostrum 5 supercomputer (6,000+ GPU-hours), stood up and benchmarked around twenty frontier model families across quantization formats, and built retrieval, evaluation, and multi-agent systems for Turkish legal-domain applications.

My approach is measurement-driven: I care about rigorous benchmarking protocols, calibration, and honest reporting of negative results. I am comfortable operating from the CUDA/driver level of multi-node GPU serving up to shipping user-facing applications.
